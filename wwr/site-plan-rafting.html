Overview

Purpose

Build Revenue - Our business started in 1999 and has grown ever since. We have more than 80 amazing itineraries ready to serve our customers. We want to expand our business using an online platform where our customers can access all the information they need to hire our services.

Audience

Our customers are individuals and families who are looking for adventure. We have family friendly activities that accommodate children as young as 4, teenagers, and adults of all ages. We have specific activities for women, men, and teenagers. These customers have a good or excellent financial situation. In the website they will find information about our various activities, separated by interest, age, and special occasions. Without the website customers that can not come in person meet our professionals find it hard to picture all the options we have, with the website we can make it much more visual and intuitive for them to decide the kind of experience they want to purchase. The customers will usually access the site on their mobile phones or laptop.
