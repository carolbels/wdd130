History

Picture this: It's the late '80s, and Rapids Rafting Company springs to life not as a formal venture but because a bunch of pals had an intense love for the great outdoors and a wild sense of adventure. These folks shared a crazy passion for the exhilaration of whitewater rafting. It all kicked off with a daring ride down a wild river that got them totally hooked. Fueled by their collective excitement and the sheer thrill of tackling rapids, they thought, "Why keep this epic experience to ourselves?" So, in 1988, Rapids Rafting Company officially set sail, starting with just one raft and a rock-solid commitment to spreading their love for the water with the world. As Rapids Rafting Company gained momentum throughout the 2000s, it became a cornerstone of the outdoor adventure scene. The founders weren't all about stiff formality; they were all about keeping things fun and safe. Over time, their fleet of rafts grew, and they welcomed more experienced guides, ensuring that anyone seeking adventure could do so without a worry. The company's commitment to eco-friendly tourism and looking after Mother Nature led to partnerships with local conservation groups, helping preserve the beautiful waters they held so dear. Today, Rapids Rafting Company isn't just a big name in rafting; it's a symbol of responsible adventure travel, inspiring tons of folks to dive into unforgettable journeys through the wild waters they call home.
